For two summer nights, the Torremolinos Bullring becomes a great Mediterranean piazza where Italian food, live music and entertainment come together to celebrate the pleasure of taking life slowly.

Little Italy Gastro Fest is more than an Italian food festival. It is a celebration of the Mediterranean pleasure of eating well, raising a glass with the people you love and enjoying a night you never want to end.

Come hungry for summer.
